Featured Courses

The classes that make this program different.

These three courses replace the older business-intelligence-only track and are the reason this degree can be called "& AI."

BUAN 3301

AI in Business

A ground-up view of how artificial intelligence is reshaping every business function — sales forecasting, hiring, service, operations — and where it still gets things wrong. Students build with LLM tools and evaluate the ethical trade-offs of every model they deploy.

LLMs Prompt Design Case Studies
BUAN 4381

Python Programming

A dedicated Python course — not a chapter inside a "tools" survey. Students move from scripting basics to writing the pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn code they will use in every downstream course and every internship in the field.

Python pandas scikit-learn
BUAN 4383

Machine Learning for Business Analytics

Regression, classification, clustering, and neural networks — taught with business-first framing. Every model students train is scored against a business outcome (revenue lift, retention, cost avoided), not just accuracy.

scikit-learn TensorFlow MLflow

Beyond the classroom

Every graduate leaves with 260+ hours of documented, real-world work.

The three components below are not optional add-ons or honors tracks — they are graduation requirements for every student in the major.

160hrs

Analytics Internship

A supervised placement with a Hampton Roads or regional employer, structured around a defined analytics deliverable — a dashboard, a model, a report the organization actually uses after the student leaves.

BUAN 4090
100hrs

Community Engagement

A social-sector engagement practicum. Students apply their analytics skills to nonprofits, city agencies, and small businesses that couldn't otherwise afford data infrastructure.

BA 4095
1capstone

Senior Capstone

A full-semester team project defined and evaluated by an industry sponsor. Culminates in a public presentation and a delivered artifact — a model, a dashboard, or a written recommendation.

BUAN 4395 / BPS 4395
