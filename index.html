Department of Information Technology & Decision Sciences

The Department of Information Technology and Decision Sciences (ITDS) is the largest academic department in the Strome College of Business. Our programs sit where three interrelated disciplines meet: Business Analytics, Information Systems & Technology, and Decision Sciences.

Students benefit from a curriculum that combines foundational business coursework with applied training in data analysis, systems design, and decision modeling. Our AACSB-accredited undergraduate and graduate programs are taught by faculty recognized nationally and internationally for their research in analytics, information systems, and quantitative business methods.

Graduates from our programs find employment at organizations including Oracle, Microsoft, Booz Allen Hamilton, Norfolk Southern, Northrop Grumman, and governmental agencies including the U.S. Navy and the Port of Virginia.
